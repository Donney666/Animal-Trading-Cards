African bush elephant

Elephant Herds Are Female Dominant. The females run and manage the herd at all times. Adult male elephants, called bulls, spend their life apart from the herd and only return when it is time to mate.

  • Scientific Name: Loxodonta africana
  • Average Height: 3.2 meters
  • Avaerage Lifespan: 60 to 70 years
  • Average Weight: 4 to 5 tons

The African bush elephant is the largest land mammal in the world and the largest of the three elephant species. Adults reach up to 7.4 meters in length and 4 meters in height and weigh up to 11 tons. As herbivores, they spend much of their days foraging and eating grass, leaves, bark, fruit, and a variety of foliage.
